The Life of "Amazing" Igala Kings(Attah's)

Note: We're presenting timeline based on years. Meaning some rulers might not appear here at the momment because of lost dates.

  • 1854

    Akwodiba

    Ruled as the Attah of Igala Kingdom from 1854 to 1870. He is from the Idoko Adegbe Rulling House.

  • 1870

    Okoliko

    He Ruled as the Attah of Igala Kingdom from 1870 to 1876. He is from the Onuche Rulling House.

  • 1876

    Amaga

    He Ruled as the Attah of Igala Kingdom from 1876 to 1900. He is from the Ekalaga Rulling House.

  • 1901

    Ochejeonokpa

    He Ruled as the Attah of Igala Kingdom from 1901 to 1903. He is from the Ameocheje Rulling House.

  • 1905

    Oboni

    He Ruled as the Attah of Igala Kingdom from 1905 to 1911. He is from the Akwodiba Rulling House.

  • 1911

    Oguche Akpa

    He Ruled as the Attah of Igala Kingdom from 1911 to 1919. He is from the Okoliko Rulling House..

  • 1919

    Ataboijomi

    He Ruled as the Attah of Igala Kingdom from 1919 to 1926. He is from the Amaga Rulling House.

  • 1926

    Obaje

    He Ruled as the Attah of Igala Kingdom from 1926 to 1945. He is from the Ochejeonokpa Rulling House.

  • 1946

    Ameh Oboni

    He Ruled as the Attah of Igala Kingdom from 1946 to 1956. He is from the Oboni Rulling House.

  • 1956

    Aliyu Obaje

    He Ruled as the Attah of Igala Kingdom from 1956 to 2012. He is from the Obaje Rulling House.

  • 2013

    Idakwo Ameh Oboni II

    The Present Attah of Igala Land. He is from the Ameh Rulling House.
